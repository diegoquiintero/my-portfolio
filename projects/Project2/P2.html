Automation of CNC for Assembly Line

Mechanical Designer & Fabricator

Designed and fabricated a custom pneumatic fixture to automate loading and unloading operations in a CNC machine as part of a robotic assembly line. This integration improved the workflow by minimizing manual intervention and increasing throughput.

The fixture was built using modular components and incorporated into the existing system with safety considerations and operator accessibility in mind.
